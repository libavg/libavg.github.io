The Special Player¶

The Special Player is an interactive performance. Contemporary dancers are augmented with a digital aura using motion tracking, resulting in complex interaction between the dancers, the VJ and the technology involved. Watch the video - it's better than trying to explain.

The Special Player was performed at Transmediale 08 - conspire, at the Fusion Festival 08 and the Piemonte Share Festival 08.

More info at http://www.thespecialplayer.com/
